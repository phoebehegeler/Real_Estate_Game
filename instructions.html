Instructions to Play

  • Choose the place that your team was given
  • You've just bought a house in your location in the year 2000
  • Now you will be in this place for the next 20 years with your house.
  • Every 4 years you have to choice to either keep or sell your house (depending on whether you think its value will increase or if you think it has reached its peak value).
  • You will get a list of factors so that you can make this decision.
  • You will get ONLY 1 minute to decide whether to keep or sell your property
  • If you do not click either of the buttons within the time limit, you will automatically keep your property
  • DISCLAIMER: After you click on sell, a popup message comes to confirm whether you want to infact sell your property. Therefore, if you click sell on the last second, then you order to sell will not go through. For it to go through, you need to click the "Yes" on the popup message.
  • Once you sell you CANNOT buy the house again.
  • Once you sell, you will be told how much money you made
  • After making the decision to keep or sell, you will see the graph of the price up until that point
  • Your objective is to make the most amount of money as possible by selling it at the perfect time.
